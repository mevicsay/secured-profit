FAQ

The answers to the most important questions on the project are collected in this section. To make it easier for you to find what you are looking for, we arranged them in the appropriate categories.

Questions regarding registration

All you need to know about the registration procedure in this project.

  1. How to register in the project?

    2. In order to register in this project, you need to click on the "Registration" button at the top of the page and fill in the required fields of the questionnaire.

  2. Who is allowed to register and participate in this investment project?

    3. Any adult citizen. The country of residence does not matter.

  3. Is the investor obligated to provide reliable information about himself?

    4. No, you can remain anonymous and decide what information to provide.

Questions regarding authorization

Questions about authorization in the project

  1. How do I access my personal cabinet?

    2. To log in, you must use the login and password that you have chosen and indicated during the registration.

  2. From which device can I access my personal cabinet?

    3. From any: phone, tablet, computer.

  3. What should I do if I forgot my password for logging in to my account?

    4. You can use the form to recover passwords or contact customer support.

Questions regarding finance

All you need to know about making deposits and withdrawing.

  1. How quickly after registration does the investor need to make a deposit?

    2. At any convenient time. After registration, you can carefully study the conditions of the service plans and only after that make the deposit.

  2. When do accrual to my deposit start?

    3. All accruals are carried out once a day. You can follow them in your personal cabinet.

  3. Is there a fee for withdrawing profits?

    4. No, all payments are instant and conducted without commissions. The minimum withdrawal amount is 0.0005 BTC.

Questions regarding partners

Information about the referral program of the investment project.

  1. How to become a member of the referral program?

    2. It is enough to be a registered participant in the project. Your referral link is stored in your personal cabinet.

  2. Do I have to have an active deposit to profit from the referral program?

    3. No, you must not.

  3. How many levels are there in the referral system?

    4. There are two levels in the referral system of our project, this will allow you to make profit from sub-referrals.
